COVID-19 Emergency Message

Ceasing Operations

County Orders Vacation Rentals to Cease Operations during COVID-19 Emergency​

Aloha...

The County of Hawai‘i has ordered vacation rentals, bed and breakfasts and time shares to cease operations and advertising during the period specified in Governor Ige’s Emergency Proclamations. The order, the Mayor’s COVID-19 Emergency Rule #1 will continue throughout the COVID-19 emergency, or until terminated sooner by the Mayor’s Order.

We shall comply with the order. We shall not accommodate guests during this emergency. We wish everyone well. Stay safe out there. We have decided to do some renovating and shall provide updates on that progress. The new and improved Kona House of Palms will welcome guests as soon as we can do so safely and in accordance to local law.

Mahalo nui loa,

Sean and Anjali
