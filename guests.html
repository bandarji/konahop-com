Guest Resources

Property Access

Address

75-103 Halahua Place, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

Directions From KOA Airport

  • Turn right/south on HI-19 (Queen Ka'ahumanu Highway).
  • HI-19 becomes HI-11 (Hawaii Belt Road) after approximately seven miles.
  • Continue south for nearly three additional miles, turn right onto Kuakini Highway.
  • Note: If you missed the turn and reached Lako, you missed Kuakini Highway.
  • In less than one mile, turn left into Kahakai Estates, on Nakukui Drive.
  • In the right lane kiosk, slowly enter the gate code.
  • Follow Nakukui Drive down the hill and curve to the right, until you see the "Road Ends" sign.
  • Just before the "Road Ends" sign, and across from the open park, turn right on Halahua Place.
  • Be aware: Some navigation systems have you turn one street too soon.
  • Park in the first driveway on your left.

Access

For the door lock, press "Yale", enter the code, then press the checkmark. Press "Yale" to lock the door.

Additional Information

Guest Booklet

A binder should sit on a living room table. It contains additional information about the house and the island. Please review at your earliest convenience. The neighbors directly across the street, Bob and Marge, will likely introduce themselves during your stay. Bob can serve as a local resource for questions about the house, community or the island, too.

Air Conditioning

Turn the Nest thermostat counterclockwise to lower the temperature setting, to start the air conditioner. When not needed, simply spin the dial clockwise to ninety degrees. The house does not have a heater, so that effectively switches off the A/C. Please do not run the A/C with any open windows or doors.

Garage

We prohibit access to the garage. That remains off-limits to guests because it contains tools and chemicals needed for house maintenance. Storage cabinets on the side of the house hold all the beach equipment, including chairs, boogie boards, toys and ice chests.

Awnings

The awnings attached to the roof of the house do not work as expected and remain unavailable for guests. We've provided multiple shade points around the house for guests to enjoy the outdoor spaces.

Books

Bring a book or two with you to the islands, to add to our bookcase. If you find yourself enjoying a book from the shelf, take it with you to finish at your convenience.
